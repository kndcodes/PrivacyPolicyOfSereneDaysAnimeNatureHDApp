Privacy Policy for SereneDays: Anime Nature HD

Effective Date: 21 October, 2025

SereneDays: Anime Nature HD (“we,” “our,” or “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information from users. Specifically:

  • No account or login information is required.
  • No names, email addresses, or phone numbers are collected.
  • No images, usage data, or preferences are sent to any external server.

All app usage — such as setting wallpapers or saving favorites — happens locally on your device.

Data Stored on Your Device

Wallpapers you set or mark as favorites are stored only on your device’s local storage. We have no access to this data, and it is deleted automatically when you uninstall the app.

Third-Party Services

We use Google AdMob to display ads within the app. AdMob may collect limited, non-personal information such as:

  • Device identifiers
  • Approximate location (for ad relevance)
  • Ad performance data

This data is handled directly by Google under their own privacy policy:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

You can manage personalized ad preferences on your device:
Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization

Permissions Used

The app requests only the permissions necessary to function properly:

  • Set Wallpaper — to apply wallpapers on your device.
  • Storage / Media Access — to save and manage wallpapers locally.

We do not access any personal photos or files outside of the app.

Children’s Privacy

This app is suitable for all age groups, including children. We do not display or provide any harmful, explicit, or adult content. Our wallpapers include only safe and family-friendly themes, such as nature and anime-style scenes. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children or any users.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will appear on this page with a revised “Effective Date.” Continued use of the app after changes indicates your acceptance of the revised policy.

Terms of Use

By using this app, you agree to use it solely for setting wallpapers for personal and non-commercial purposes. The wallpapers provided within the app are intended for use as device backgrounds only. Downloading, redistributing, or reselling wallpapers from this app is not permitted. We reserve the right to update or modify the app or this policy at any time without prior notice.
